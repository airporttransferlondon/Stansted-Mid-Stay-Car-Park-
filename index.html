What Is Stansted Airport Mid Stay Parking?

stansted mid stay car park is a secure, budget-friendly parking option located just a short distance from the terminal. Ideal for travelers seeking a balance between affordability and convenience, this car park is operated by Manchester Airports Group (MAG) and offers frequent shuttle transfers directly to the terminal.

Unlike the Short Stay car park (which is steps from check-in but pricier), Mid Stay is slightly farther but offers considerable savings—especially for longer trips. It’s perfect for families, solo travelers, or business passengers looking to cut travel costs without compromising on reliability.

Stansted Airport Mid Stay Parking Costs

Pricing for Stansted Mid Stay varies based on trip duration and how far in advance you book. As of 2025:

  • 1 day: From £10–£15
  • 1 week: From £45–£60
  • 2 weeks: From £75–£90

Early bookings (3+ weeks ahead) usually secure the lowest rates. Last-minute parking can cost up to 2–3x more. Prices also fluctuate based on season, demand (e.g., school holidays), and availability.

How to Book Mid Stay Parking at Stansted

Booking is straightforward through the official Stansted Airport website. Here’s how:

  1. Visit the parking section and select “Mid Stay.”
  2. Enter your arrival and departure dates.
  3. Compare real-time prices and choose your slot.
  4. Complete secure payment and receive a confirmation email with your booking reference.

Always keep your booking reference handy—you’ll need it when you arrive. Pre-booking guarantees your space and locks in the best rate.

Shuttle Bus Service Details

A free, 24/7 shuttle bus connects Mid Stay to the terminal every 10–15 minutes. The journey takes approximately 8–10 minutes.

Buses are wheelchair-accessible and run frequently even during night hours. Look for clearly marked pickup zones in both the car park and terminal arrivals area. Real-time updates are displayed on digital boards at the bus stops.
